Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, larger only than Mercury. In the English language, Mars is named for the Roman god of war.

Information about Mars
Distance from Sun 227.9 million km
Surface area 144.8 million km²
Age 4.603 billion years
Mass 6.39 × 10^23 kg
Radius 3,389.5 km
Gravity 3.721 m/s²
